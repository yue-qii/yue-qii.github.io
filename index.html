I am a Senior Research Engineer in Standards and Mobility Innovation Lab, Samsung Research America since Fall 2022. My current research is focusing on Wi-Fi standard. I obtained my Ph.D. from Villanova University under the supervision of Prof. Mojtaba Vaezi.

Research Interests

  • Wi-Fi Standard

  • Wireless Communications

  • Signal Processing

  • Non-Orthogonal Multiple Access (NOMA)

  • Physical Layer Security

  • Time-frequency Analysis

  • Signal Classification and Identification
